Pokémon Crystal Legacy: Timeless

An RTC-free, MBC5 fork of Pokémon Crystal Legacy.

MBC5 · NO RTC

Crystal Legacy was never designed to run on cartridges without an RTC, so on those it quietly corrupts your save. This patch fixes that, and stacks a bunch of quality-of-life improvements on top. Check it out.
